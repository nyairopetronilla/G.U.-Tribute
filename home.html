About the Garissa University Attack

On 2 April 2015, the Garissa University in Kenya experienced a tragic terrorist attack that resulted in the loss of many innocent lives. This website is dedicated to honoring the memory of the victims and supporting their families.

Remembering the Victims

We remember and honor the lives of those who were taken too soon. Their memories live on in our hearts and minds. This website serves as a place of remembrance and solidarity.
