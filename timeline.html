Timeline

This timeline provides an overview of the events before, during, and after the Garissa University attack on April 2, 2015.

  • April 1, 2015: Students prepare for end-of-term exams.
  • April 2, 2015: Attack occurs early in the morning.
  • April 3, 2015: Memorial services begin across the country.
  • April 4, 2015: International community expresses solidarity.
